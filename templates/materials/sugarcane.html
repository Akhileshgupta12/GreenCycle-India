Sugarcane Bagasse

₹3 – ₹6 per kg

Sugarcane bagasse is the fibrous residue left after extracting juice from sugarcane. It is a highly valuable eco-friendly material widely used in industries like paper, biofuel, packaging, and organic farming. Instead of burning it, this waste can be transformed into profitable and sustainable products.

♻️ Common Uses

Paper Manufacturing
Biofuel & Energy
Disposable Plates
Eco Packaging
Organic Compost

🌱 Benefits

  • Eco-friendly and biodegradable
  • Reduces pollution and waste burning
  • High demand in industries
  • Generates extra income for farmers
